Ogden Water Filtration

Clean, purified water for Ogden and Weber County. Residential and commercial systems from PureFlow, installed and serviced locally.

Ogden's water supply comes from the Ogden River, Pineview Reservoir, and deep wells throughout Weber County. The treatment plants do solid work meeting federal guidelines, but the dissolved mineral content tells a different story at the tap. Weber County residents commonly see TDS readings between 200 and 400 ppm, with hardness levels that rival anywhere else in Utah.

The effects are visible in most Ogden homes: white crusty buildup around faucets, spotted glassware from the dishwasher, dry skin and hair after showers, and a subtle mineral taste in drinking water. These aren't just aesthetic issues — hard water forces your water heater to work harder, clogs showerheads, and accelerates wear on every water-using appliance in your home.

PureFlow serves Ogden, Roy, Layton, Clearfield, North Ogden, and surrounding Weber and Davis County communities. Our systems are sized for your household, calibrated for local water conditions, and backed by ongoing maintenance from a technician who knows the area.
