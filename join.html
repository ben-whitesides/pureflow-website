The Opportunity Nobody's Talking About

Here's something most people don't realize about Utah tap water. It passes federal standards. That sounds reassuring until you learn those standards haven't been updated in almost 20 years. The EWG found 89 contaminants in Utah water systems. Arsenic at 135 times above health guidelines. Bromochloroacetic acid at 168 times.

That's 3.5 million people drinking water that technically "passes" but doesn't come close to what independent health organizations consider safe.

Now here's where it gets interesting for you. Every one of those households is a potential customer. And every business with a water cooler that still uses bottles or a basic filter is leaving money on the table — yours.

PureFlow reps don't cold call. They show up with a free TDS water test, let people see the number on the meter, and ask one question: "Would you like to fix that?" The product sells itself. The water test closes the deal.

And unlike most sales gigs where you get paid once and move on, PureFlow pays you every month for as long as that customer has the system. Place 100 residential accounts? That's $1,500 a month hitting your bank whether you work or not. Stack commercial accounts on top of that and the math gets real interesting, real fast.
