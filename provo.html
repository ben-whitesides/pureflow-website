Provo Water Filtration

Clean water for Provo and Utah County. Residential and commercial systems installed and serviced by a local PureFlow technician.

Provo's water supply draws from a combination of Deer Creek Reservoir, the Provo River, and underground wells across Utah County. The treatment process meets EPA standards, but Utah County is known for having some of the hardest water along the entire Wasatch Front. Residents in Provo, Orem, Springville, and Spanish Fork regularly see TDS readings between 300 and 500 ppm.

Hard water is more than a nuisance here — it's a real cost. Scale buildup reduces water heater efficiency by up to 30%, shortens the life of dishwashers and washing machines, and leaves white mineral deposits on every surface that touches water. For families in Utah County, a quality water softener paired with an RO system is the most effective combination.

PureFlow serves Provo, Orem, Lindon, Pleasant Grove, Springville, Spanish Fork, and the surrounding Utah County communities. Every installation is performed by Parker, our South Territory technician who knows Utah County's water conditions firsthand.
