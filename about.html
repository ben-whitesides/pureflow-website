Hard Water

The Wasatch Front has some of the hardest water in the western US. Calcium and magnesium cause scale buildup in pipes, water heaters, and appliances — shortening their lifespan and increasing your energy costs.

🧪

High TDS Levels

Utah tap water regularly exceeds 350 ppm total dissolved solids. Chlorine, fluoride, nitrates, and trace metals that affect taste, smell, and long-term health. Federal standards haven't been updated in nearly 20 years.

📍
