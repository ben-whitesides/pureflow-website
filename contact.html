Frequently Asked Questions

Reverse osmosis pushes water through a semi-permeable membrane that filters out up to 99% of dissolved solids, including chlorine, fluoride, lead, arsenic, nitrates, and microplastics. Our systems reduce TDS from 350+ ppm (typical Utah tap water) to under 20 ppm.
Most under-sink RO installations take 60 to 90 minutes. Whole house systems and water softeners typically take 2 to 3 hours. Our technicians handle everything — you don't need to prep anything beyond clearing space under the sink or near your main water line.
Pre-filters (sediment and carbon) are replaced every 6 to 12 months. The RO membrane itself lasts 2 to 3 years depending on usage and water quality. All filter changes are included in your monthly plan — we schedule them for you and handle the swap.
All PureFlow systems include a manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. RO systems come with a 5-year warranty on the membrane and a 1-year warranty on other components. Whole house and softener systems have comparable coverage. We also stand behind our installation work.
